# Sovereign Drive

> **Legally accountable AI for autonomous vehicle operation**  
> A Convergence Human & Technology research initiative

---

## Overview

`sovereign-drive` is a private R&D initiative exploring the design and 
formal validation of AI systems capable of assuming legal and operational 
responsibility for autonomous vehicle control.

The target reliability threshold is **99.9999%** a six-nines standard 
applied not only to uptime, but to **decision correctness under all 
certified operating conditions**.

This repository is the public-facing reference point for the project.  
Core technical implementations, training pipelines, and certification 
frameworks remain confidential.

---

## Research Scope

- Formal accountability models for AI decision-making in mobility contexts
- Safety-critical system architecture for autonomous agents
- Legal liability frameworks adapted to AI-driven vehicle operation
- Fault-tolerance and zero-accident operational guarantees
- Human-AI interaction at handover boundaries

---

## Design Philosophy

> "An AI that cannot be held accountable should not be trusted with 
> a steering wheel."

The project treats **legal responsibility as a first-class engineering 
constraint** not a post-deployment consideration. Every architectural 
decision flows from the question: *can this system formally account for 
its actions in a court of law ?*
